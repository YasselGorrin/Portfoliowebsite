ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Yassel Gorrin. I am a Graphic Designer and Front-End Developer based in Miami, Florida. I've studied graphic design since high school and graduated from New World School of the Arts with my bachelor's in 2025 and have been working in the field for the last 5 years. I hold creativity & innovation to a high regard and think design/art has the power to change the world. My mission as a designer is to connect people and inspire others through my work. I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow as a designer. When I'm not designing, I'm typically engaging in varieties of physical activities, reading, or spending quality time with my loved ones.
